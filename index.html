My Favorite Animal

The Bald Eagle

The Bald Eagle has been my favorite animal for as long as I can remeber. I completed a report on the Bald Eagle way back in the 3rd grade and remember some of the things I included in the report. The Bald Eagle is not only the patriotic symbol of the United States, but it is one of the leading birds of prey throughout North America. The Bald Eagle has a wingspan up to 80.3 inches andcan weigh up to 105-222 ounces. The Bald Eagle gets its famous white head and brown plumage after about 5 years.

    Fun Eagle Facts
  • Eagles are at the top of the food chain
  • An eagle's eyesight is around 5 times better than a human's vision
  • There are more than 60 eagle species
  • Eagles can fly as high as 15,000 feet
  • Eagles can fly up to 99 miles per hour
